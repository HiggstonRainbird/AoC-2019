## Poem: Symmetry

They dance the music of the spheres  
As epochs pass away.  
And when the tune repeats itself  
So do their dance, and they.  
 
Their chorus has no earthly sound;  
We have but earthly ears.  
We only hear an echo of  
The music of the spheres.
